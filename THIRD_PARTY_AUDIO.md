# Third-Party Audio

Jazz Chart Lab includes selected Salamander Grand Piano MP3 samples and can load the
following additional sample presets on demand when a sampled tone is selected.

- Salamander Grand Piano V3: 120 samples arranged as 30 pitches (roughly every
  minor third) across four velocity layers for the `Salamander Grand Piano 4-Layer`
  tone. The selected velocity layers are 3, 7, 11, and 15 from the original
  16-layer library. They are distributed as 48 kHz stereo MP3 at 160 kbps and
  trimmed to 7.2 seconds for browser decoding and mobile playback.
  Source: https://archive.org/details/SalamanderGrandPianoV3
  Browser sample packages: https://github.com/darosh/samples-piano
  Author: Alexander Holm.
  License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported.
- Finger Bass YR: selected fingered electric bass samples for the `Finger Bass YR` tone.
  Source: https://freepats.zenvoid.org/ElectricGuitar/clean-electric-bass.html
  Author: Andrea Biasior.
  License: CC0 1.0 Universal.
- MuldjordKit: selected kick, snare, closed/open hi-hat, ride, ride bell, crash,
  and four tom samples for the `Muldjord Jazz Kit`, `Muldjord Fusion Kit`, and
  `Muldjord Studio Kit` tones.
  Source: https://freepats.zenvoid.org/Percussion/acoustic-drum-kit.html
  Recorded by Lars Muldjord; assembled for FreePats by Roberto.
  License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International.
- GeneralUser GS: sampled acoustic bass.
  Source: https://github.com/mrbumpy409/GeneralUser-GS
  License: GeneralUser GS License v2.0.
- WebAudioFontPlayer: browser sample playback engine.
  Source: https://surikov.github.io/webaudiofont/
  License: GPL-3.0-or-later.

The original sample banks and the WebAudioFontPlayer project retain their respective
copyrights and licenses. This file is included so the provenance of every sampled
tone used by the published application remains visible.
