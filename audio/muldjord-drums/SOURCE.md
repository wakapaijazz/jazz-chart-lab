# MuldjordKit Browser Sample Selection

These files are selected and converted samples from MuldjordKit, recorded by
Lars Muldjord and assembled for FreePats by Roberto.

- Source: https://freepats.zenvoid.org/Percussion/acoustic-drum-kit.html
- Source release: MuldjordKit SFZ+FLAC, version 2020-10-18
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International
- Included instruments: kick, snare, closed/open hi-hat, ride, ride bell,
  crash, and four tom pitches

The selected FLAC recordings were converted to 160 kbps MP3 for browser
playback. The original recordings and this selection remain under CC BY 4.0.
