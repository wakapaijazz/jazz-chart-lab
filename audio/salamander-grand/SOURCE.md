# Salamander Grand Piano 4-Layer

These samples are derived from Salamander Grand Piano V3 by Alexander Holm.

- Original source: https://archive.org/details/SalamanderGrandPianoV3
- Browser sample packages: https://github.com/darosh/samples-piano
- Original sample license: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported
- Included velocity layers: 3, 7, 11, and 15
- Included pitches: 30 positions sampled roughly every minor third
- App encoding: 48 kHz stereo MP3, 160 kbps, 7.2 seconds maximum duration

The files were converted from the Ogg Vorbis browser sample packages without
normalization so the recorded differences between velocity layers are retained.
